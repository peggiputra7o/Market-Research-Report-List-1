<p><p>The Offshore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) market is categorized into three types: shallow, medium, and large. Shallow-water AUVs operate in depths of up to 200 meters, primarily used for coastal surveys and environmental monitoring. Medium AUVs function in depths between 200 to 1,500 meters, suited for oceanographic research and underwater inspections. Large AUVs can venture beyond 1,500 meters, often utilized for deep-sea exploration, oil and gas industry applications, and military operations, showcasing advanced capabilities and versatility.</p></p>
