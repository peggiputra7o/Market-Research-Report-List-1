<p><strong>The Hemodialysis Apparatus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pump</li><li>Double Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis apparatus market is divided into two main types based on the pump configuration: single pump and double pump systems. Single pump systems utilize one pump to manage both blood flow and dialysate circulation, offering a compact and cost-effective option ideal for smaller facilities. In contrast, double pump systems have separate pumps for blood and dialysate, allowing for greater flexibility and efficiency in treatment, making them suitable for larger healthcare settings requiring higher performance and advanced features.</p></p>
