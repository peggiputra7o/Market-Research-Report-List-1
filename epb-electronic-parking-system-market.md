<p><p>The Electronic Parking System (EPB) market features two main types: cable-pull type and electric-hydraulic type. The cable-pull type relies on mechanical cables to engage and disengage the parking brake, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, the electric-hydraulic type utilizes electronic controls and hydraulic force for operation, providing enhanced precision and comfort. Both systems aim to improve vehicle safety and convenience, but they cater to different automotive designs and user preferences, driving their unique market dynamics.</p></p>
