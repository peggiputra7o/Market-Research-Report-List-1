<p><ul><li>Absorption Exhaust Muffler</li><li>Reactive Exhaust Muffler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine exhaust system muffler market comprises primarily two types: absorption exhaust mufflers and reactive exhaust mufflers. Absorption mufflers reduce noise by using sound-absorbing materials, which help dampen sound waves as exhaust gases pass through. Conversely, reactive mufflers utilize internal chambers and baffles to reflect sound waves, creating destructive interference that minimizes noise levels. Both types are essential for meeting noise regulations and enhancing vehicle performance, catering to diverse automotive applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
