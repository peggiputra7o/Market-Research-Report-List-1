<p><p>The freshwater fishing rod market comprises primarily two types: spinning rods and casting rods. Spinning rods are lightweight and designed for use with spinning reels, making them suitable for beginners and versatile for various species. They offer ease of use and better control of lighter baits. Casting rods, on the other hand, are built for baitcasting reels and allow for precise casting, making them ideal for targeting larger fish. Both types cater to different fishing techniques and angler preferences, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
