<p><p>Segmented diamond saw blades are categorized into three main types based on their manufacturing processes. Sintered blades, created through high-temperature pressure, offer durability and high cutting efficiency for various materials. Welded blades involve bonding diamond segments to a steel core using welding techniques, providing strength and stability during cutting. Electroplated blades, known for their precision, feature a layer of diamond particles attached to a base through electroplating, ideal for cutting hard materials with intricate details. Each type serves unique industrial cutting needs.</p></p>
