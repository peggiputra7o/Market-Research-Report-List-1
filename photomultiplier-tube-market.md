<p><p>A Photomultiplier Tube (PMT) is a highly sensitive light detection device that amplifies the signal produced by incident photons. It operates on the principle of the photoelectric effect, converting light into an electrical current through a series of dynodes that multiply the electrons generated by initial photon absorption. PMTs are widely utilized in applications such as medical imaging, nuclear and particle physics, environmental monitoring, and various scientific research fields due to their exceptional sensitivity and fast response time.</p><p>The Photomultiplier Tube Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in sectors like healthcare and scientific research.
