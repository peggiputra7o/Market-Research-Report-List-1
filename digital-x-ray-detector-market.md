<p><p>The digital X-ray detector market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless detectors. Wired detectors are connected directly to imaging systems via cables, offering stable connectivity and consistent image quality. However, they may limit mobility and flexibility in positioning. Conversely, wireless detectors utilize Wi-Fi or other wireless technologies, allowing for greater maneuverability and easier patient access. This wireless nature enhances workflow efficiency but may present challenges in maintaining signal integrity and battery life. Both types serve critical roles in modern medical imaging.</p></p>
