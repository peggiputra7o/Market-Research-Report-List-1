<p><strong>The Automotive Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermistor</li><li>Resistance Temperature Detector</li><li>Thermocouple</li><li>IC Temperature Sensor</li><li>MEMS Temperature Sensor</li><li>Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive temperature sensors market comprises various types, each serving distinct functions. Thermistors are cost-effective and offer rapid response, making them popular in engine applications. Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs) provide high accuracy and stability for critical measurements. Thermocouples handle extreme temperatures and are commonly used in exhaust systems. IC temperature sensors offer compact form factors and digital output. MEMS temperature sensors deliver miniaturization and reliability for various automotive systems. Infrared sensors enable non-contact measurements, useful for both internal and external vehicle applications.</p></p>
