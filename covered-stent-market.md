<p><p>The covered stent market primarily comprises two main types: Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm (AAA) stent grafts and Thoracic Aortic Aneurysm (TAA) stent grafts. AAA stent grafts are designed for the treatment of abdominal aortic aneurysms, providing a minimally invasive solution to reinforce weak artery walls. In contrast, TAA stent grafts address aneurysms in the thoracic aorta, ensuring structural support and reducing the risk of rupture. Both types enhance patient outcomes by addressing complex vascular conditions effectively and safely.</p></p>
