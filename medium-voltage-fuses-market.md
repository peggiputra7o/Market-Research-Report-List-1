<p><p>The medium voltage fuses market comprises several types, primarily including current limiting fuses, expulsion fuses, and others. Current limiting fuses are designed to quickly interrupt excessive currents, protecting equipment from damage. Expulsion fuses rely on a mechanism that expels the melted material to extinguish the arc during fault conditions. Other types include blade fuses and design variations that cater to specific applications. Together, these fuses ensure reliable protection and system stability in medium voltage electrical networks.</p></p>
