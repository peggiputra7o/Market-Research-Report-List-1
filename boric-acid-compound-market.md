<p><p>The Boric Acid Compound market comprises two primary types: Anhydrous Borate and Borate with Water. Anhydrous Borate is a dry, crystalline form that is highly concentrated, making it suitable for industrial applications such as glass manufacturing and pesticides. In contrast, Borate with Water is a hydrated form, often used in applications requiring additional moisture, such as in pharmaceuticals and as a buffer in chemical processes. Each type serves distinct purposes based on their chemical properties and water content.</p></p>
