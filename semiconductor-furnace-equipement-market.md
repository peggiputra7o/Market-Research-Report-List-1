<p><p>The semiconductor furnace equipment market encompasses three primary applications: oxidation, annealing, and diffusion. Oxidation involves forming a silicon dioxide layer on a wafer, crucial for device insulation and passivation. Annealing is used to repair damage to the crystal structure after ion implantation, improving electrical properties. Diffusion introduces dopants into the silicon substrate, controlling the electrical characteristics of the semiconductor. Together, these processes are vital for producing high-performance semiconductor devices in various applications, from consumer electronics to telecommunications.</p></p>
