Semi-automatic machines require some human intervention, offering a balance between efficiency and control. Fully automatic machines streamline the entire filling process, maximizing production speed and reducing labor costs. Manual machines rely entirely on operator input, suitable for small-scale operations or specialized formulations. Each type caters to different production needs and scales in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.</p></p>
