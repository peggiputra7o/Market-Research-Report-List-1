<p><p>The Decorative Plastic and Paper Laminates market is divided into two primary types: Low Pressure Laminates (LPL) and High Pressure Laminates (HPL). Low Pressure Laminates are created by bonding decor paper with a thermosetting resin at lower pressures, resulting in a lightweight and cost-effective option suitable for surfaces like furniture and cabinetry. In contrast, High Pressure Laminates involve applying heat and pressure to multiple layers, creating a durable, highly resistant surface ideal for countertops, flooring, and high-traffic areas.</p></p>
