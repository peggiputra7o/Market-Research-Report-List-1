<p><p>The UHD 4K panel market consists of three primary types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD panels utilize liquid crystals and a backlight, offering good color accuracy but limited contrast. LED panels enhance LCD technology with improved backlighting, resulting in brighter images and better energy efficiency. OLED panels feature organic light-emitting diodes, providing exceptional color richness, deeper blacks, and wider viewing angles due to their self-emissive nature. Each technology caters to different consumer preferences and use cases within the UHD 4K landscape.</p></p>
