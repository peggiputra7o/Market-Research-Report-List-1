<p><p>The heavy machinery market comprises three main vehicle types: complete vehicles, incomplete vehicles, and semitrailer vehicles. Complete vehicles are fully assembled machinery ready for operation, like excavators or bulldozers. Incomplete vehicles serve as chassis or platforms for further customization, often requiring additional components or attachments. Semitrailer vehicles consist of towed units that connect to a tractor unit, commonly used for transporting goods or equipment. Each type caters to specific operational needs within construction, mining, and logistics industries.</p></p>
