<p><p>The stereo microscopes market is primarily categorized into binocular and trinocular types. Binocular stereo microscopes feature two eyepieces, offering a three-dimensional view, making them ideal for tasks requiring depth perception, such as dissection or circuit board inspection. Trinocular microscopes, on the other hand, come with an additional third eyepiece, allowing for simultaneous photography or video recording while maintaining the dual eyepiece functionality. This feature is particularly useful in research and documentation, enhancing versatility in various applications.</p></p>
