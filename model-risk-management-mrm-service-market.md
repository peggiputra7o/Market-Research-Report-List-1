<p><p>Model Risk Management (MRM) encompasses various types to address risks associated with financial models. Model Specification Risk Management focuses on the accuracy and relevance of model design assumptions. Model Implementation Risk Management deals with the risks arising during the development and coding of the model, ensuring it functions as intended. Model Application Risk Management evaluates how models are utilized in decision-making and operational processes, addressing potential inaccuracies in predictions and their impact on business outcomes.</p></p>
