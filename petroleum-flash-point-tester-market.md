<p><strong>The Petroleum Flash Point Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cup Flash Point Tester</li><li>Closed Cup Flash Point Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petroleum flash point tester market encompasses two primary types: open cup and closed cup flash point testers. Open cup testers measure the flash point by exposing the sample to air in an open container, allowing for the most accurate assessment of volatility at atmospheric pressure. In contrast, closed cup testers contain the sample in a sealed environment, minimizing evaporation and providing results that reflect conditions closer to real-world scenarios. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the safety and quality of petroleum products.</p></p>
