<p><p>The glass primer market primarily includes silane and urethane glass primers. Silane glass primers enhance adhesion between glass and substrates by creating covalent bonds, making them ideal for automotive and construction applications. Urethane glass primers, on the other hand, offer flexibility and durability, supporting strong bonds in high-stress environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring optimal performance of adhesives and sealants, while also addressing environmental concerns through the development of low-VOC formulations for safer application.</p></p>
