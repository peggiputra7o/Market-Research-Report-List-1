<p><strong>The ToF Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF Sensors</li><li>Indirect ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ToF (Time-of-Flight) image sensor market is divided into two main types: Direct ToF sensors and Indirect ToF sensors. Direct ToF sensors measure the time it takes for emitted light to bounce back from an object, providing precise distance information, making them ideal for applications like 3D mapping and gesture recognition. Indirect ToF sensors, on the other hand, analyze the phase shift of modulated light to estimate distance, which is often used in less complex applications like automotive and consumer devices for depth sensing.</p></p>
