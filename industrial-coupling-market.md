<p><p>The industrial coupling market comprises various types designed to connect shafts and transmit torque in machinery. Snake spring couplings offer flexibility and shock absorption, making them ideal for misalignment scenarios. Gear couplings provide high torque capacity and are robust, suitable for heavy machinery. Tire couplings, made from elastomeric materials, enhance damping and reduce vibration, while diaphragm couplings use a flexible diaphragm to accommodate misalignment and provide efficient torque transmission. Each type serves specific applications across industries.</p></p>
