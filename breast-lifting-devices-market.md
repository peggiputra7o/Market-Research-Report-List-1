<p><p>The breast lifting devices market includes various techniques used for breast elevation and enhancement. The Crescent Breast Lift involves a small incision and is ideal for minimal sagging. The Benelli Lift, or donut lift, uses a circular incision around the areola for a natural look. The Benelli-Lollipop combines features of both, creating a lollipop-shaped incision for more significant lift. Full Mastopexy offers extensive reshaping for severe sagging, providing substantial elevation and a youthful contour. Each method caters to different aesthetic needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
