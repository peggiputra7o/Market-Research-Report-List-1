<p><p>Flexible type epoxy resins encompass several market segments, including urethane modified, rubber modified, and dimer acid-based resins. Urethane modified epoxy resins offer enhanced flexibility and durability, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Rubber modified epoxy resins provide superior impact resistance, ideal for applications requiring shock absorption. Dimer acid epoxy resins, derived from natural sources, enhance flexibility and are often used in coatings, offering improved resistance to chemicals and environmental factors. Each type caters to specific performance needs in various industries.</p></p>
