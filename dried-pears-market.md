<p><p>The dried pears market primarily consists of baked dried and freeze-dried varieties. Baked dried pears are produced by slowly dehydrating the fruit in low heat, enhancing their natural sweetness and flavor while resulting in a chewy texture. In contrast, freeze-dried pears undergo a quick freezing process followed by low-pressure drying, preserving their shape, color, and nutritional value. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with baked dried offering a robust taste and chewy experience, while freeze-dried provides a lightweight, shelf-stable option.</p></p>
