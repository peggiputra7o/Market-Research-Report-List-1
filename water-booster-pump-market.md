<p><strong>The Water Booster Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water booster pump market is segmented into single stage and multiple stage pumps based on design and application. Single stage pumps consist of one impeller, making them suitable for low-pressure boosting and simple systems. In contrast, multiple stage pumps feature several impellers, allowing for higher pressure output and efficiency in demanding applications like high-rise buildings or industrial processes. The choice between them depends on the specific needs for pressure, flow rate, and system complexity.</p></p>
