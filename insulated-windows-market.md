<p><p>The insulated windows market comprises various types designed to enhance energy efficiency and comfort. Secondary glazing adds an extra layer of glass for improved insulation. Tinted glass reduces heat and glare by absorbing solar energy. Double and triple glazing consist of multiple glass panes with insulating air spaces, enhancing thermal performance. Low-emissivity coating glazing features a thin metallic layer that minimizes heat loss. Reflective coating glazing reduces solar heat gain by reflecting sunlight, thereby optimizing indoor temperatures.</p></p>
