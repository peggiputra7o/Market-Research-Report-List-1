<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Brushed Direct Current Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Wound Brush DC Motors</li><li>Permanent Magnet Brush DC Motors</li><li>Shunt Wound Brush DC Motors</li><li>Compound Wound Brush DC Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brushed direct current (DC) motor market is categorized into four main types. Series wound brush DC motors are characterized by their motor windings in series with the armature, offering high starting torque. Permanent magnet brush DC motors utilize permanent magnets for the field, ensuring compactness and efficiency. Shunt wound brush DC motors have windings connected in parallel, providing stable speed under varying loads. Compound wound brush DC motors combine features of series and shunt types, enabling versatility and improved performance in various applications.</p></p>
