<p><p>The Handheld Iris Recognition Scanner market is categorized into two main types: Optical Iris Scanners and Electric Iris Scanners. Optical Iris Scanners capture high-resolution images of the iris using visible or near-infrared light, relying on software algorithms for recognition. In contrast, Electric Iris Scanners utilize electrical signals to analyze the iris's unique patterns. Both technologies serve diverse applications in security, healthcare, and access control, offering efficiency and accuracy in biometric identification while catering to varying user needs and preferences.</p></p>
