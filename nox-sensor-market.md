<p><p>The NOx sensor market features two primary types: Five Needles and Four Needles sensors. Five Needles sensors utilize an enhanced design with five electrode needles, offering improved measurement accuracy and response times for nitrogen oxides in exhaust systems. In contrast, Four Needles sensors employ a simpler configuration, maintaining reliable NOx detection at a potentially lower cost. Both types cater to automotive and industrial applications, with their specific designs catering to varying performance requirements and regulatory standards in emissions control.</p></p>
