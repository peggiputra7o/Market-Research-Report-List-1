<p><p>The Surface Safety Valves market encompasses two primary types: Hydraulic Safety Valves and Pneumatic Safety Valves. Hydraulic Safety Valves utilize fluid pressure to detect and respond to abnormal conditions, providing effective protection against overpressure situations. In contrast, Pneumatic Safety Valves operate using compressed air or gas, enabling rapid response to pressure changes. Both types are crucial in maintaining safety and operational efficiency in various industries, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation, ensuring equipment and personnel are protected from hazardous situations.</p></p>
