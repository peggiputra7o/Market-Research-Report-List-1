<p><strong>The Digital Paper Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Digital Paper Hygrometers</li><li>Relative Digital Paper Hygrometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital paper hygrometers market is divided into two primary types: absolute and relative digital paper hygrometers. Absolute digital paper hygrometers measure the total moisture content present in the air, providing precise readings in absolute terms. In contrast, relative digital paper hygrometers focus on the percentage of moisture in the air compared to the maximum amount of moisture the air can hold at a specific temperature. Both types are essential for applications requiring accurate humidity measurement, such as in preservation and storage industries.</p></p>
