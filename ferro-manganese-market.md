<p><p>The Ferro Manganese market is categorized into three types based on carbon content: High Carbon, Medium Carbon, and Low Carbon. High Carbon Ferro Manganese (HCFeMn) contains 6-8% carbon and is primarily used in steelmaking, enhancing strength and hardness. Medium Carbon Ferro Manganese (MCFeMn) contains 0.5-2% carbon, balancing properties for special steel applications. Low Carbon Ferro Manganese (LCFeMn), with less than 0.1% carbon, is used in producing high-quality stainless steel and low-alloy steel, minimizing the carbon footprint.</p></p>
