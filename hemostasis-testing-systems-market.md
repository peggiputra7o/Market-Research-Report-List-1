<p><p>Hemostasis testing systems are crucial for diagnosing and managing bleeding disorders. The market is divided into two main types: congenital bleeding disorders and acquired bleeding disorders. Congenital bleeding disorders, such as hemophilia, stem from genetic factors that affect blood clotting. In contrast, acquired bleeding disorders arise from external factors, including medications, liver disease, or vitamin deficiencies. Both segments require specialized testing to assess coagulation function, guiding treatment decisions and improving patient outcomes in managing bleeding risks.</p></p>
