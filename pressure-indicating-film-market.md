<p><strong>The Pressure Indicating Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3LW and 4LW Pressure Indicating Film</li><li>LW and LLW Pressure Indicating Film</li><li>MW and MS Pressure Indicating Film</li><li>HS and HHS Pressure Indicating Film</li><li>5LW Pressure Indicating Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure Indicating Film (PIF) is categorized based on sensitivity and pressure measurement capabilities. The 3LW and 4LW films are designed for low to moderate pressures, while LW and LLW films are suited for low pressure applications. MW and MS films cater to medium pressures, offering a balance between sensitivity and reliability. HS and HHS films are engineered for high and higher-high pressures. Lastly, 5LW films provide the highest sensitivity for ultra-low pressure measurements, catering to specialized applications requiring precise data.</p></p>
