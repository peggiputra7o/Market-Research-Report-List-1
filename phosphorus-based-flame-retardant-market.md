<p><strong>The Phosphorus-based Flame Retardant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Additive Flame Retardants</li><li>Reactive Flame Retardants</li><li>Synergist Flame Retardants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphorus-based flame retardant market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **Additive Flame Retardants** are mixed with materials without chemical bonding, providing protection through a physical presence. </p><p>2. **Reactive Flame Retardants** are chemically bound within polymers, integrating flame resistance directly into the material's structure. </p><p>3. **Synergist Flame Retardants** enhance the effectiveness of other flame retardants by improving their performance or reducing the required concentration, optimizing functional properties and overall safety in applications.</p></p>
