<p><p>The Pipeline Pigging Systems market comprises several types, including Ultrasonic Pigging (UT) and Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL). Ultrasonic Pigging utilizes ultrasonic waves for precise measurement and inspection of the pipeline interior, enhancing the detection of flaws and blockages. Magnetic Flux Leakage, on the other hand, leverages magnetic fields to identify corrosion and defects in metal structures. Other market segments encompass various technologies, like mechanical and cleaning pigs, each aimed at ensuring pipeline efficiency and integrity.</p></p>
