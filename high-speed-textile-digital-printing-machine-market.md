<p><p>The high-speed textile digital printing machine market is primarily divided into two types: rotary screen and flat screen. Rotary screen printing utilizes cylindrical screens that enable continuous printing on textiles, ideal for large-scale productions with intricate designs and vibrant colors. In contrast, flat screen printing employs flat screens, allowing for detailed and versatile prints, suited for smaller runs and diverse fabrics. Both methods offer unique advantages, catering to different production needs within the textile industry while enhancing efficiency and design capabilities.</p></p>
