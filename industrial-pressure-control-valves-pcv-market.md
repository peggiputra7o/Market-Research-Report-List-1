<p><p>The Industrial Pressure Control Valves (PCV) market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into Pneumatic Control Valves, Electric Control Valves, and Other Types. Pneumatic control valves utilize compressed air to regulate pressure, offering quick response and reliability. Electric control valves employ electric actuators, providing precise control and ease of integration with automation systems. Other types include manual and hydraulic control valves, catering to specific industrial needs. Collectively, these valves play a crucial role in managing fluid pressure in various applications.</p></p>
