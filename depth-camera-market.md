<p><strong>The Depth Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured-Light Camera</li><li>Stereo Vision Camera</li><li>Time-of-flight Camera</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The depth camera market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Structured-Light Cameras** project a known pattern of light onto a scene to calculate depth by analyzing the distortion of the pattern.  </p><p>2. **Stereo Vision Cameras** use two or more lenses to capture images from different angles, triangulating depth based on the disparity between the images.  </p><p>3. **Time-of-Flight Cameras** measure the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, providing real-time depth data.  </p><p>4. **Others** include various emerging technologies and hybrid systems that enhance depth perception.</p></p>
