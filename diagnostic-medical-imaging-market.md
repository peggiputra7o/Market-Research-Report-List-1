<p><p>The Diagnostic & Medical Imaging market encompasses various imaging technologies critical for accurate diagnosis. X-ray imaging systems provide quick visualization of bones and tissues. Computed Tomography (CT) scanners offer detailed cross-sectional images, enhancing diagnostic precision. Ultrasound systems employ sound waves to visualize soft tissues, making them vital for obstetric and abdominal assessments. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) systems deliver high-resolution images of internal structures using magnetic fields. Nuclear imaging systems use radioactive substances to examine organ functions. Other technologies may include advanced modalities like fluoroscopy and digital mammography.</p></p>
