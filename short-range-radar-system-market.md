<p><p>The Short Range Radar System market comprises primarily two types: Continuous Wave (CW) Radars and Pulsed Radars. CW Radars emit a continuous signal, providing real-time speed and distance measurements, ideal for applications like automotive collision avoidance. Pulsed Radars, on the other hand, transmit discrete pulse signals, measuring target distances with high accuracy and are commonly used in industrial and security systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing safety, efficiency, and automation across various sectors.</p></p>
