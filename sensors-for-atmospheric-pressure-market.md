<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sensors for atmospheric pressure market can be categorized into analog output and digital output types. Analog output sensors provide a continuous voltage or current signal proportional to the pressure measurement, facilitating straightforward integration with traditional recording devices. In contrast, digital output sensors convert pressure readings into discrete numerical values, often transmitted via protocols like I2C or SPI, ensuring higher accuracy and ease of data processing. Both types are essential for various applications, including weather monitoring, aviation, and industrial processes.</p></p>
