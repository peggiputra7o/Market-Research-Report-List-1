<p><p>Composite springs are innovative spring materials made from high-performance composite materials, typically incorporating polymers reinforced with fibers such as carbon or glass. Additionally, a growing focus on sustainability and energy efficiency is encouraging industries to adopt composite materials, which often yield lower energy consumption over their lifecycle.
